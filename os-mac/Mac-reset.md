# Mac reset

## Mac reset smc

> Ref: <https://support.apple.com/en-us/102605>

1. Shut down your Mac.

1. Keep Press these keys for 7 seconds, then release all keys and the power button at the same time. (Mac might turn on now)

1. Keep press another 7 seconds, then release all keys and the power button at the same time.

## Mac reset system

> Note: Use this method only if you have no important data or do not need a backup.
>
> Ref: https://b23.tv/xptFSxa

1. **Erase Content and Settings:** Go to Settings > General > Transfer or Reset > Erase All Content and Settings. Wait for the system to shut down.

1. **Enter Recovery Mode:** The system will automatically enter a black recovery screen. Click the menu in the top-left corner and select Erase Mac.

1. **Follow On-Screen Prompts:** Once the Mac is erased, follow the system prompts. It will default to installing the original factory version of the operating system.
